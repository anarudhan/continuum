## My Top 3 Recommendations

### 🥇 Concept 7: Mobius Memory
**Why:** The Möbius strip perfectly visualizes "continuum" — infinite, continuous, one-sided. The gradient from cyan to lavender is distinctive. It works at any size (favicon to billboard). Most memorable.

### 🥈 Concept 1: Memory Mesh
**Why:** Immediately communicates "network of agents sharing memory." The hexagonal node pattern is recognizable and scalable. Strong for developer tools.

### 🥉 Concept 2: Infinite Loop
**Why:** The clever "C = ∞" integration is memorable. Typography-driven logos age well. Works great in monochrome and at small sizes.
